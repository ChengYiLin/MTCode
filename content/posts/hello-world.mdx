## Why MTCode?

Every engineer eventually starts a blog. This is mine.

MTCode is where I'll document things I learn, opinions I form, and tools I find useful. Writing forces clarity — if I can't explain something, I probably don't understand it well enough yet.

## What to Expect

Expect posts about:

- **Web development** — Next.js, React, TypeScript, and the modern frontend ecosystem
- **Developer tooling** — editors, CLIs, workflows, and anything that saves keystrokes
- **Software craft** — architecture decisions, code review, and engineering culture
- **Occasional deep dives** — when I go down a rabbit hole and want to share what I found

## The Stack

This blog is built with:

- [Next.js 16](https://nextjs.org) with the App Router
- [Tailwind CSS v4](https://tailwindcss.com)
- [shadcn/ui](https://ui.shadcn.com) for components
- MDX via `next-mdx-remote` for post content
- Deployed on [Vercel](https://vercel.com)

Posts live as `.mdx` files in `content/posts/`, with frontmatter for metadata. No database, no CMS — just files.
